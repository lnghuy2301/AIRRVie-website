The AIRRVie Solution: A Comprehensive AI System

To address complex challenges, AIRRVie is built as an intelligent, personalized system that acts as a trusted agricultural advisor for each farming household.

Experience the intuitive interface and powerful features of AIRRVie.

View Product

AIRRVie's Workflow

1

Multi-Source Data Integration

The system aggregates data from satellites, weather information, hydrology, and data provided by farmers themselves.

2

AI-Powered Analysis & Forecasting

Advanced AI models analyze the aggregated dataset to assess crop health and forecast risks.

3

Personalized Recommendations

The virtual assistant provides specific, easy-to-understand farming recommendations via a user-friendly mobile interface.

Core Features and Benefits

Smart & Optimized Farming

Receive precise recommendations on planting schedules, irrigation plans, and proper fertilizer use to optimize resources and production costs.

Crop Health Management

Early detection of pest and disease signs through image analysis, allowing for timely intervention and minimizing the use of chemical pesticides.

Risk & Yield Forecasting

The AI system helps forecast climate risks like drought and salinity, and estimates end-of-season yields, helping farmers be more proactive.

Sustainable Farming

Integrated greenhouse gas (GHG) emission tracking tools encourage environmentally friendly practices, helping to protect soil health and ecosystems.

Intuitive & Accessible Interface

Built on the NuocGPT platform, the virtual assistant can interact through various means (text, images), ensuring all farmers can use it easily.

Comprehensive Community Support

More than just a tool, AIRRVie focuses on Gender Equality and Social Inclusion (GESI) and provides information on policies and financial support to empower the community.

AIRRVie

A collaborative research project aimed at empowering Vietnamese farmers through Artificial Intelligence, fostering sustainable agriculture and climate change adaptation.
