News & Events

Update with the latest information, activities, and important milestones of the AIRRVie project.

Event May 12, 2025

Consultation Workshop on "AI Application for Climate-Resilient Rice Farming in Vietnam"

Exploring the impact of weather on rice production through an integrated gender and stakeholder approach to identify advantages and challenges for farmers, thereby building an AI assistant tool tailored to their needs. The meeting identified farmers' urgent needs for AI in price forecasting, hydrometeorology, and technical support for pest management.

News November 18 & 20, 2025

Field Data Collection Survey in the Mekong Delta

Field results at Co Khia Cooperative (An Giang) recorded challenges such as falling rice prices, rising input costs, limited production infrastructure, and a heavy labor burden on women. In Nhu Gia commune (Can Tho), where an organic rice model is piloted, difficulties were noted regarding high material costs, lack of trade linkages, and incomplete disease management.

Event November 21, 2025

Stakeholder Workshop for the AIRRVie Project

Introducing the Proof-of-Concept (PoC) version of the AI Assistant and gathering constructive feedback on technical and policy aspects to support farmers in transitioning to sustainable farming. Delegates practiced testing the AI Assistant and held group discussions to assess the suitability of AI features and propose technical/operational requirements.
