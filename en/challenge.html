Chuyển sang tiếng Việt

Core Challenges

Vietnam's rice agriculture, while a cornerstone of the economy, faces urgent issues that need to be addressed.

Pressure from Climate Change

With over 3,260 km of coastline and low-lying deltas, Vietnam is extremely vulnerable. Drought, saltwater intrusion, and unusual floods in the Mekong Delta have caused an estimated $674 million in damages from 2014-2016, directly threatening the livelihoods of over 70% of the region's population.

Limitations of Traditional Farming

Current farming methods have many limitations. Over 90% of the 8 million farming households still rely on the excessive use of chemical fertilizers and pesticides. This not only increases costs but also harms the environment, degrades soil health, and affects long-term crop quality.

The Technology Gap

Although many digital tools exist, they have not truly reached Vietnamese farmers, with only 3% using specialized apps. These tools are often generic and lack personalization. This is the gap that AIRRVie was created to fill.

AIRRVie

A collaborative research project aimed at empowering Vietnamese farmers through Artificial Intelligence, fostering sustainable agriculture and climate change adaptation.
